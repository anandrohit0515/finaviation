Who We Are

Where Finance Meets the Skies

Rigorous, independent analysis at the intersection of aviation and personal finance

Photo by Unsplash — Free to use

FinAviation was founded on a simple observation: the aviation industry is full of financial decisions — and almost no one is writing about them clearly. We exist to change that. Our mission is to deliver honest, data-driven analysis on the economics of private flight, aviation investing, and the business of air travel — written for people who take their finances as seriously as they take their flying.

What We Cover

FinAviation sits at a unique crossroads. We are not a lifestyle blog, and we are not a trade publication. We are a finance-first editorial platform with aviation as our lens.

✈️

Aircraft Ownership

The real economics of buying, operating, and exiting private jets — from light aircraft to ultra-long-range cabin jets.

📈

Aviation Investing

Stocks, ETFs, leasing companies, and infrastructure plays — how to build exposure to the aviation sector intelligently.

🏦

Industry Finance

Airline balance sheets, fuel hedging, fleet financing, and the macroeconomic forces shaping commercial aviation.

🧭

Smart Alternatives

Fractional ownership, jet cards, and charter economics — rigorous comparisons to help you fly smarter, not just more.

The Team Behind FinAviation

FinAviation is written and edited by a team of finance professionals and aviation enthusiasts who believe that better information leads to better decisions — in the air and on the ground.

✦

The FinAviation Editorial Team

Finance & Aviation Analysts

Our contributors bring backgrounds in corporate finance, investment analysis, and commercial aviation. Every article published on FinAviation is researched independently, written with analytical rigour, and reviewed for accuracy before publication. We have no paid partnerships with aircraft manufacturers, charter operators, or financial institutions — our only obligation is to our readers.

Our Editorial Values

  • 🔍
    Independence We accept no sponsored content, paid placements, or affiliate arrangements that influence our editorial positions. Every opinion expressed on FinAviation is our own.
  • 📊
    Data-Driven Analysis We cite our sources, show our working, and present cost figures as estimates with appropriate context — not as marketing copy designed to impress.
  • ⚖️
    Balanced Perspective Private aviation is not right for everyone. We present the full financial picture — including the cases where owning a jet makes no economic sense.
  • 🔄
    Accuracy & Updates Markets change. Fuel prices shift. Tax laws evolve. We review and update our articles regularly to ensure the information remains current and reliable.
