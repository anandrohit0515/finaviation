1. Who We Are

FinAviation ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at https://finaviation.com. We publish editorial content covering the intersection of personal finance and aviation, including articles on aircraft ownership costs, aviation investing, and related topics.

For any privacy-related questions, you may contact us at: [email protected]

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address, if you sign up for our newsletter or launch notification list
  • Any information you include when contacting us via email

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address and general geographic location (country/region level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This automatically collected data is gathered via cookies and standard web analytics tools (see Section 4).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the FinAviation website and its content
  • Send launch notifications or newsletters to subscribers who opt in
  • Respond to your enquiries or messages
  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. FinAviation uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand visitor behaviour (e.g., Google Analytics). These are anonymous and aggregated.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements based on your browsing history. These are third-party cookies controlled by Google.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality or the relevance of ads shown to you.

For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

5. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

FinAviation participates in the Google AdSense program to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

What this means for you: Google may use information about your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. This is called interest-based advertising.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or by visiting aboutads.info.

FinAviation has no access to or control over the cookies used by Google or other third-party advertisers. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We comply with Google AdSense programme policies, including requirements around privacy disclosure and user consent where applicable.

6. Third-Party Links

Our articles may contain links to third-party websites, including financial services providers, aviation companies, and news sources. These links are provided for reference and informational purposes only.

FinAviation has no control over the content, privacy practices, or policies of third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any site we link to. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy:

  • Email subscribers: Your email is retained until you unsubscribe. Every email we send will include an unsubscribe link.

  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymised — retained per the default Google Analytics retention period (26 months).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.
